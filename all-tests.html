All Diagnostic Tests

Complete Blood Count (CBC)

Evaluates overall health by measuring red cells, white cells, and platelets.

White Blood Cell (WBC) Count

Assesses immune system function by counting white blood cells.

Platelet (PLT) Count

Measures platelets to evaluate blood clotting ability.

Red Blood Cell (RBC) Count

Counts red blood cells to diagnose anemia or other conditions.

Blood Sugar Test

Monitors glucose levels to diagnose or manage diabetes.

Urine Test

Analyzes urine for infections, kidney function, or other disorders.

Hemoglobin Test

Measures hemoglobin levels to assess anemia or oxygen transport.

Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate (ESR)

Detects inflammation in the body.

SGOT (AST)

Evaluates liver and heart function through enzyme levels.

SGPT (ALT)

Assesses liver health by measuring enzyme levels.

Pregnancy Test

Detects pregnancy through hCG hormone levels.

Typhoid Test

Diagnoses typhoid fever caused by Salmonella typhi.

Thyroid Function Test

Measures thyroid hormone levels to assess gland function.

Malaria Test

Detects malaria parasites using microscopy or rapid tests.

C-Reactive Protein (CRP) Test

Measures inflammation levels to diagnose infections or chronic diseases.

Stool pH Test

Assesses digestive health by measuring stool acidity or alkalinity.

Stool Hanging Drop Test

Detects motile bacteria like Vibrio cholerae in stool for infection diagnosis.

Urine Sugar and Protein Test

Measures glucose and protein in urine to assess diabetes and kidney health.
