Directions to Hindustan Laboratory

  • By Road: Thalarka is well-connected via Nohar to Rawatsar Road, located near the Water Works.
  • By Public Transport: Regular buses and auto-rickshaws operate from Hanumangarh to Thalarka. Alight at Thalarka Bus Stand and walk 2 minutes to our laboratory.
  • Landmarks: Look for the Water Works and Thalarka Community Health Center; our laboratory is a short walk from these landmarks.
